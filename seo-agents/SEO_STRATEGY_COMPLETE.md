## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive, Day-1 SEO strategy for erateapp.com, a SaaS platform providing E-Rate consulting services for schools and libraries. The strategy focuses on:

- **Low-competition, high-intent keywords** (Position 8-20 opportunities)

- **Conversion-optimized title tags and H1s**
- **Strategic internal linking** to pass authority to money pages

### Link Flow Rules

1. **Blog posts → Service pages** (pass authority UP to money pages)
2. **Resource pages → Audience pages → Homepage** (funnel flow)
3. **Service pages → Related services** (cross-sell within silo)
4. **All pages → Contact/CTA** (conversion path)
