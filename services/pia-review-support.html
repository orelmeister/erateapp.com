What is PIA Review?

PIA (Program Integrity Assurance) is USAC's process for verifying that E-Rate applications comply with program rules before approving funding.

Why Does PIA Exist?

USAC reviews applications to ensure they follow competitive bidding rules, contain accurate information, and request only eligible services. PIA protects taxpayer dollars and program integrity.

When Does It Happen?

PIA review occurs after you submit Form 471 and before funding is approved. You'll receive questions via the EPC portal and email‚Äîrespond within 15 business days!

Why Is It Critical?

Incomplete or incorrect PIA responses lead to funding denials. Even approved applicants can face denials due to poor PIA responses months after filing.

You have only 15 business days to respond to each PIA question. Missing this deadline can result in automatic denial. Extensions are rarely granted‚Äîdon't wait!
