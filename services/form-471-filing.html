What is Form 471?

Form 471 is your official funding request to USAC. It details the services you need, the vendors you've selected, and the discounts you're requesting.

‚ö†Ô∏è No Extensions Granted

The FCC does not grant extensions for Form 471 filing. If you miss the deadline, you lose an entire year of funding. Don't wait until the last week.

Funding Request Numbers (FRNs)

Each service or group of services gets its own FRN on your Form 471. We ensure FRNs are structured properly to maximize approval chances.

Contract Documentation

Your 471 must accurately reference your contracts and match your Form 470 competitive bidding. Mismatches trigger PIA review questions.

Entity Information

Every building receiving services must be listed with correct addresses, enrollment numbers, and NSLP eligibility data.
