E-Rate Consulting LLC was founded by Richard Bernstein, MA, SDA, MSC, SUB, CEMP, a New York State Certified School District Administrator and a doctoral candidate at the New York University Graduate School of Education. With over 30 years of experience in school administration, Richard has been actively involved with the E-Rate program since its inception in 1998.
A ten-year member of EMPA, the premier association of E-Rate consultants, Richard has earned their certification as an expert in the E-Rate process.
Over the years, he has helped schools secure millions of dollars in new funding, recover hundreds of thousands of dollars in previously denied funds, and successfully navigate on-site audits and fund recovery proceedings. Most importantly, he guides schools through the E-Rate process in a way that ensures minimal scrutiny of their applications.
Richard’s guiding principle is simple:
"Applications that are done properly—within both the explicit rules and the unwritten expectations of the program—move through the process smoothly."
