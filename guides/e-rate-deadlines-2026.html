Form 470: Competitive Bidding Deadlines

The Form 470 initiates the competitive bidding process. Key requirements:

  • 28-Day Rule: Form 470 must be posted for at least 28 days before selecting a vendor
  • No Deadline: Form 470 can be filed any time, but earlier is better
  • Best Practice: File Form 470 by early November to allow time for bidding before Form 471 window
